The Oxford Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions (BDS) Coalition is a coalition of staff and students at the University of Oxford committed to Palestinian liberation. We stand in full solidarity with the student-led [Oxford Action for Palestine (OX4P) Solidarity Encampment](https://linktr.ee/oxact4pal). Please see our collective demands below:

1. Disclose University-wide Assets
2. Divest University-wide Assets 
3. Overhaul Investment Policy
4. Boycott Institutional Relationships
5. Drop Barclays 
6. Rebuild and Reinvest
